Our Background

Daisy started Good Gums in 2007. Daisy has always loved plants and being in the garden, and it was through this passion that she started Good Gums. Over her years designing outdoor environments, Daisy found that it was strangely difficult to source quality Australian natives, which sparked the beginning of Good Gums.

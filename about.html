Small Beginnings

Good Gums is a completely local Australian owned and founded company. The genesis of Good Gums was Daisy's garage. Determined to propagate natives in Australian gardens Daisy started growing seedlings from seeds she initally collected from the wild. These seedlings were sold at first out of her garage (right). These monthly garage sales soon became fortnightly, which became weekly trips to farmer's markets. The business kept growing and growing - from a sapling to a great Acacia.

Our Commitment to Natives

Lots of our customer ask us: "Why do you specialise in natives? What made you want to focus on that area?"
The answer is, like many things, a combination of different reasons. First of all, we love Australian wildlife. The bush landscape is one that is truly unique in the world. If you need some evidence, check out our products page. It was this passion for the wildlife itself that made us want to strive to bring it closer to people. We really want to make it easier for everyday people to get their hands on natives easily. We want to see Australian gardens look more like Australia.

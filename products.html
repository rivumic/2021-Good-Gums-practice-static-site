Small Plants

Tall Kangaroo Paw Anigozanthos Flavidus Growing up to a meter long this hardy plant flowers from late spring all through summer.
Canberra Bells Correa Federation Fell x Mannii This pretty shrub grows to a height of 1m and produces lovely red and white flowers in autumn.
Grevillia Firecracker Grevillea Alpina x Rosmarinifolia This easy to maintain shrub grows to a height of 0.5m and has colourful red and yellow flowers from autumn right through until spring.
Poa Grass Poa Labillardierei Poa labillardierei is a native grass that can grow to a height of approximately 120cm and looks especially attractive around water features

Large Plants

Rose Boronia Boronia Serrulata This shrub will grow up to 1 m in height and does best in well drained soils. Pretty pink flowers are produced during the spring.
Happy Wanderer Hardenbergia Violacea With its pretty purple flowers and quick rate of expansion this climbing plant is ideal for covering pergolas, fences, and trellises.
Lilly Pilly Syzygium Smithii Growing to a height of 3-5m grown together these plants can be used for hedging. It has pretty flowers in the summer and fruits in winter.
Waratah Telopea Speciosissima This evergreen shrub grows to a height of 3m. It displays astounding red flowers throughout spring.

Trees

Golden Wattle Acacia Pycnantha A tree that grows to a height of 10m it delights with vibrant yellow flowers in spring.
Rusty Gum Angophora Costata This tree is best known for its colourful peeling bark. It grows up to 30m in height and has pretty white flowers.
Crimson Bottlebrush Callistemon Citrinus This tough plant is great to use as hedging and displays wonderful red flowers from spring through to summer.
Red Flowering Gum Corymbia Ficifolia This flowering gum will grow to a size of around 3-4 m and produces.
Silver Princess Gum Eucalyptus Caesia A native of Western Australia this gum grows to between 6-8m and can live for up to 150 years.
Snow Gum Eucalyptus Pauciflora Native to eastern Australia the Snow Gum grows to a height of 5-7m and will put up with some frost
Silky Oak Grevillia Robusta This graceful tree can grow to a height of between 10-30m and produces wonderful orange and gold flowers in spring.
